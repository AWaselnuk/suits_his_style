I would like to become a client

Suits his Style operates by appointment only. If you would like to obtain services from Suits his Style, first you need to access services from, and be referred by, one of our referral agencies. Current referral agencies are listed below and new agencies will be added as they become eligible, so please check back often. If you are working with an agency who is not on this list and you think they should be, send us an email at info@suitshisstyle.ca.
